int item::ItemProperties::cooldown () const

Accessor for the cooldown of the item.

The cooldown in general represents the amount of energy needed to trigger an action in the item, though it can represent other quantities too. Essentially, the item is used by reducing its cooldown value. The cooldown usually increases by 1 per turn if the player leaves an item alone.

Returns
Cooldown quantity.
